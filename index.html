About Me

Hello! My name is Jordan McClintock and I am a senior at Northern Illinois University. My major area of study is computer science with an emphasis on enterprise software and my minor area of study is business administration. I will be graduating this May and I am currently looking for full time employment. I have experience programming in all types of environments and I enjoy learning new technologies. I have experience developing applications on the command line, using winforms in visual studio, web applications, and even on an IBM mainframe computer. I enjoy programming in each of these styles equally because the part I enjoy most about programming is solving problems and thinking critically.

When I am not programming I enjoy spending time with my girlfriend, friends, and family. I am a huge fan of superheroes, television, and rap music. My favorites include the following: Batman, Breaking Bad, and Chance the Rapper.
